# TopScore Golf - Installation Guide

## How to Install on Your Phone

TopScore Golf is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works like a native app but installs directly from your web browser - no app store needed!

---

## iPhone (iOS) Installation

1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone
2. Navigate to: `https://topscore.vercel.app`
3. Tap the **Share button** (⬆️) at the bottom of the screen
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. (Optional) Edit the app name
6. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
7. The TopScore Golf icon will appear on your home screen

**Important Notes:**
- Must use **Safari** browser (not Chrome or other browsers)
- After installation, launch from the home screen icon for the full app experience

---

## Android Installation

1. **Open Chrome** on your Android device
2. Navigate to: `https://topscore.vercel.app`
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top right
4. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
5. Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"** to confirm
6. The TopScore Golf icon will appear on your home screen

**Alternative:**
- Look for an install banner at the bottom of the page
- Tap **"Install"** if the banner appears

---

## What You Get

Once installed, TopScore Golf will:
- ✅ Work offline (after first use)
- ✅ Run in fullscreen mode without browser UI
- ✅ Automatically update when new versions are available
- ✅ Feel like a native app
- ✅ Access your camera to scan scorecards

## Troubleshooting

**"Add to Home Screen" option not showing (iPhone)?**
- Make sure you're using Safari, not another browser
- Try refreshing the page first
- Make sure you're on the main page (not a subpage)

**Installation not working (Android)?**
- Make sure you're using Chrome browser
- Try visiting the site in an Incognito window
- Check that Chrome is up to date

**App not working offline?**
- Visit the app at least once while online first
- The service worker needs to cache resources on first visit
